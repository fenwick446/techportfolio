TRAIN ROTOSCOPE
This is my train rotoscope. This was made using adobe animate. To make it I used a echnique callled rotoscoping. This is where on different layers you trace over each individual frame, later removing the video when all frames have been completed leaving you with just the outline. This turns into your own animation
